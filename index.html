Oneiroi: Personifications of Dreams

Long ago, the Ancient Greeks believed in many gods. The most well known gods are Zeus, Poseidon, and Hades. However, there are gods that are not well known; gods that come from the shadows of the night. These dream gods are known as oneiroi (oneiros for singular). Some believed that there were 1000 of them; all children of Hypnos: the god of sleep. But only 3 are known. Phobetor: the god of nightmares, Phantasos, the god of illusions, and Morpheus: the winged god of dreams. Not much is known about these gods, but that is what makes them truly fascinating.
